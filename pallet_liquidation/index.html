Liquidate pallet

Overview

This pallets provides offchain worker to call the liquidate_borrow operation in loans pallet. The collator may opt-in with a pre-funded account. The liquidate strategy is:

  • find the unhealthy account which has exceeded loans
  • liquidate the currency with higher loans
  • liquidator gets any of the affordable collaterals.
